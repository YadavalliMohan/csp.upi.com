Abstract

It is a survey conducted to find how many people are using UPI payments in an area. For this survey we selected B.V.Nagar village, in SPSR NelloreDistrict. Our main theme is to know how many people are using UPI payments and the majorpeople using which payment app in the area and also explain the advantages of using UPI payments to the people who are not utilising it. We collected the data from various people and found out how many people were using UPI payments and the major UPI payment app in that area.
